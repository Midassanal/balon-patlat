How to play

Tap the colorful balloons to pop them and earn points. Pop the target color for bonus points, chain pops for combos, grab power-ups, and avoid the black balloon. Beat a giant boss balloon every 5th level and climb the global leaderboard.

Frequently asked questions

How do I change the language?
Use the EN / ES / 中文 / TR buttons at the top of the start screen. The game also opens in your device language automatically.

How does the leaderboard work?
Enter a nickname and pick your country, then your best scores appear on a worldwide leaderboard. You can play offline too — scores sync when you're online.

How do I turn off the sound?
Tap the speaker icon (🔊) in the top-right corner during the game.

How do I remove my leaderboard entry?
Email us at gokceemrah@gmail.com with your nickname and approximate score, and we'll remove it.

Does the game have ads or purchases?
The game is free and shows ads (non-personalized). There are no in-app purchases.
